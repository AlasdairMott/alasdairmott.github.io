CrayonBrush

Drawing

The brush tool draws and erases crayon strokes.

Steps

  1. Click and drag to start drawing a stroke.
  2. Release to end the stroke.

Erasing

The eraser tool erases strokes.

Steps

  1. Hold the left control key while clicking and dragging.

Brush Size

The brush size can be changed by clicking and draggind while holding down CTRL and ALT

Steps

  1. To increase the brush size, while holding down CTRL and ALT click and drag the mouse to the right.
  2. To decrease the brush size, while holding down CTRL and ALT click and drag the mouse to the left.

Draw Lines

Crayon brush can draw straight lines

Steps

  1. Activate the brush tool in draw mode.
  2. Click on the starting point of the line.
  3. While holding down shift, click the point where you would like the line to end.

Perspective Snap

Crayon brush can snap to the XYZ directions of the active CPlane

Steps

  1. Activate the brush tool in draw mode.
  2. Click to start drawing.
  3. While the mouse is pressed, move the cursor in the direction of the axis you want to snap to.
  4. Drag the mouse to draw a line along snapped axis.
  5. Release the mouse to end the line.
